About Hardwood Mills Inc.

01 Our History

Hardwood Mills Inc. has been serving the Millington, MD area for over 20 years, providing high-quality lumber and related products.

02 Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best lumber products and services at competitive prices, while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.

03 Our Products

We offer a wide range of products including industrial grade lumber, mulch, veneer logs, crane mats, pallet stock lumber, and rough cut lumber.

04 Our Commitment

We are committed to providing excellent customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our clients.
